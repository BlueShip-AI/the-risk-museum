The Tulip Wing · a nightly bubble watch · updated 2026-07-20

Stocks that have doubled in the past two years

Named for tulip mania: in 1637, at a bulb auction in Haarlem, Holland, buyers simply stopped bidding — and history’s most famous bubble collapsed. Every night this wing screens our universe for stocks fitting the historical profile of a mania — a two-year run-up of 100% or more (the Harvard screen of Greenwood, Shleifer & You, 2019).
